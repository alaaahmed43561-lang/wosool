Wosool

A platform to help people with disabilities and beginners use online services easily

About Wosool

Wosool is designed to help people with disabilities, elderly users, illiterate users, and anyone who struggles with using the internet. The platform explains online services step by step in a very simple way.

Available Services Training

  • How to create and use email
  • Using government service websites
  • Online appointments and forms
  • Basic internet safety

Accessibility Features

  • Clear steps
  • Large readable text
  • Suitable for beginners
